Privacy Policy

XOFade — Last updated: March 16, 2026

XOFade ("the app") is a tic-tac-toe game developed by Gunduz Ozan. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Data We Collect

The app itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Your preferences (theme, game mode, difficulty) are saved locally on your device only and never leave it.

Advertising

The app displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve ads, including device identifiers and usage data, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Ad serving follows standard (non-child-directed) settings.
